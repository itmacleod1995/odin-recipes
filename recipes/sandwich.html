Better Peanut Butter Sandwich

Description

A simple peanut butter sandwich, but it replaces jelly with applesauce. Kids love it and it has less sugar.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons applesauce

Directions

  1. Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread, and applesauce on the other slice. Place them together, cut in half, and serve
